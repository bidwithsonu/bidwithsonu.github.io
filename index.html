About BID WITH SONU

BID WITH SONU is a professional procurement and engineering consultancy platform supporting consultants, contractors, and organizations in successful tender participation and project development. We provide complete solutions for Tender Bidding Support, EOI & Proposal Documentation, DPR Preparation, BOQ & Cost Estimation, Engineering Design, and Procurement Consultancy Services. Our mission is to simplify the bidding process, improve proposal quality, and help clients achieve better opportunities through smart and strategic solutions.
